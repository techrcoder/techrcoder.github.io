Assisty

2022–Present In Beta
AI / ML SwiftUI iOS

An intelligent productivity app for iPhone, iPad, and Mac, built to learn how you actually work, not how a calendar assumes you do.

Assisty is a SwiftUI app that blends natural language processing, regression, and self-correcting algorithms to learn user habits over time. Instead of asking users to plan around rigid calendar blocks, it observes how a schedule actually gets used and adjusts its suggestions accordingly.

The project has been running since 2022. The beta launched in 2024 and has reached 1,000 users; iterative fixes cut the crash rate by 85%, and design changes for student workflows lifted daily task completion by 12%. A full public release is targeted for Winter 2026, and most of the work between now and then is refinement: tightening the habit-learning models and the onboarding experience ahead of a wider launch.
