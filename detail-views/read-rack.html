Read-Rack

May 3–11, 2025 Shipped
SwiftUI Core Data Vibe-Coded

A reading tracker for iPhone, entirely vibe-coded with ChatGPT and Cursor: idea to App Store in about a week.

Read-Rack was designed to be a simple, easy-to-use app that helps readers track their progress and manage their reading lists. It's built on the native SwiftUI and Core Data frameworks, with a clean, modern interface.

Users can create and manage reading lists, track progress book by book, and set reading goals. The app also includes a built-in timer to help readers stay focused during a session.

Available on the App Store, designed for iPhone.
