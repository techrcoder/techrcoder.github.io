Vibe-Coding Proj #1: Read-Rack for iPhone

May 2025 (~1 week from design to MVP to release.) Coding Project Hobby Work

Read-Rack was an entirely vibe-coded app using ChatGPT & Cursor, between May 3rd and May 11th, 2025.

It was designed to be a simple, easy-to-use app that allows users to track their reading progress and manage their reading lists. The app was built on the SwiftUI and Core Data native frameworks, and it features a clean and modern user interface.

Read-Rack allows users to create and manage their reading lists, track their reading progress, and set reading goals. The app also includes a built-in timer that helps users stay focused while reading.
